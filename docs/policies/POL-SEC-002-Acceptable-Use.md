# POL-SEC-002 — Acceptable Use Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 · **Effective:** 2026-06-07 · **Owner:** HR / ISMS Lead · **Review:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

Define acceptable and prohibited use of Trancendos systems, networks, and data.

## 2. Scope

All personnel with access to Tranc3, Forgejo, vault, observability stacks, and customer environments.

## 3. Acceptable use

Users may access systems only for authorised business purposes, using assigned credentials, from approved devices, and in compliance with all Magna Carta policies.

## 4. Prohibited activities

- Sharing credentials or bypassing authentication
- Accessing data without business need (curiosity browsing)
- Installing unapproved software on systems handling production data
- Circumventing rate limits, Ice Box, or compliance controls
- Storing customer PII on personal devices or unencrypted media
- Using production data in non-production without anonymisation
- Cryptomining, torrenting, or illegal content on company infrastructure
- Social engineering or unauthorised penetration testing
- Retaliatory action against good-faith security reporters

## 5. Monitoring

Trancendos may monitor system usage, access logs, and communications on company systems to the extent permitted by law and disclosed in privacy notices.

## 6. Reporting

Report policy violations or suspicious activity to Security Ops or ISMS Lead immediately.

## 7. Enforcement

Violations may result in access suspension, investigation, and termination of contract or employment.
