# POL-PRI-001 — Data Protection & Privacy Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 · **Effective:** 2026-06-07 · **Owner:** DPO · **Review:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensure processing of personal data complies with UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and DPA 2018.

## 2. Scope

All personal data processed by Trancendos as controller or processor via Tranc3.

## 3. Principles (Art. 5)

Personal data shall be processed lawfully, fairly, transparently, for specified purposes, minimised, accurate, stored limitedly, securely, and accountably.

## 4. Lawful basis

Processing requires documented lawful basis in ROPA before collection. Consent must be freely given, specific, informed, and withdrawable.

## 5. Data subject rights

Trancendos honours all data subject rights within statutory timeframes via PROC-DSR-001.

## 6. Privacy by design

New features require DPO consultation. PIAs mandatory for high-risk processing (profiling, special categories, large-scale monitoring).

## 7. Security

Technical measures per POL-SEC-001 and GDPR-ALIGNMENT.md (encryption, access control, pseudonymisation).

## 8. Breach notification

Personal data breaches assessed and reported per GDPR Arts. 33–34 and PROC-IR-001.

## 9. International transfers

No transfer outside UK/EEA without appropriate safeguards (adequacy, SCCs, or UK IDTA) and DPO approval.

## 10. Processors and sub-processors

Written contracts (Art. 28) required. Sub-processors listed in supplier register; customers notified per contract.

## 11. Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| DPO | Privacy programme, ROPA, breach decisions |
| ISMS Lead | Security controls supporting privacy |
| Engineering | Privacy-by-design implementation |
| All staff | Report breaches; handle data minimally |
