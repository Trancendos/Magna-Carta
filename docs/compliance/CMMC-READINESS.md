## 1. Position

Trancendos is **not** a defence contractor processing CUI. CMMC applies if Tranc3 processes **Controlled Unclassified Information** for DoD supply chain customers.

---

## 2. Level assessment (if triggered)

| CMMC level | Typical requirement | Tranc3 starting point |
|------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Level 1 | 17 practices, self-assessment | Many practices met via DEFSTAN/ISO |
| Level 2 | NIST 800-171 110 practices | Gap assessment required |
| Level 3 | NIST 800-172 + government assessment | Not planned without contract |

---

## 3. Activation checklist

- [ ] Customer contract specifies CUI and required CMMC level
- [ ] CUI boundary and flow-down clauses in agreement
- [ ] SPRS score preparation (if Level 2)
- [ ] Physical/media controls if CUI on endpoints (POL-SEC-003)
- [ ] Incident reporting to DoD timelines
