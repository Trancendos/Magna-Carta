## 1. Applicability

Activate when Trancendos or Tranc3 customers process:

- University research data under **UK Concordat to Support Research Integrity**
- **UKRI** or **NIHR** funded research with data management plans
- **Clinical trial** or **NHS research** data (may also trigger NHS DSPT)

---

## 2. Concordat principles mapping

| Principle | Control |
|-----------|---------|
| Rigour and integrity | PROC-CHG-001, audit trail, model cards |
| Transparency | AI-GOVERNANCE, model documentation |
| Data management | ROPA, retention in POL-PRI-001 |
| Collaboration | Access controls PROC-IAM-001 |
| Accountability | RACI, incident reporting |

---

## 3. Research data handling requirements

| Requirement | Artefact |
|-------------|----------|
| Data management plan alignment | Customer contract schedule |
| Ethics approval reference | Customer responsibility; platform logs access |
| Pseudonymisation / segregation | Tenant isolation, vault encryption |
| Secure transfer | TLS, encrypted exports |
| Destruction at study end | PROC-DSR-001 deletion workflow |

---

## 4. Activation checklist

- [ ] Research customer identified
- [ ] DMP reviewed against Tranc3 capabilities
- [ ] ROPA row for research processing
- [ ] BAA/DPA or university DSA signed
- [ ] Concordat compliance attestation in contract
