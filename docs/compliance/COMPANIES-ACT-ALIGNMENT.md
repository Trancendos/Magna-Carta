## 1. Purpose

Documents how Trancendos meets **corporate governance** obligations under the Companies Act 2006 at programme level. This is not a substitute for statutory filings (Companies House) or qualified legal/accounting advice.

**Programme status:** ✅ Governance framework, director duties awareness, and record-keeping requirements documented. Statutory filings remain Finance responsibility (🎯 external validation via filed accounts).

---

## 2. Director duties (s.171–177) — programme mapping

| Duty | Summary | Magna Carta / Town Hall control |
|------|---------|--------------------------------|
| s.171 | Act within powers | BoardRoom charter; shareholder/member agreements |
| s.172 | Promote company success | Strategic decisions documented in BoardRoom minutes |
| s.173 | Independent judgment | Conflict of interest declaration process |
| s.174 | Reasonable care, skill, diligence | Competence via role definitions (COMPLIANCE-BLUEPRINT) |
| s.175 | Avoid conflicts of interest | Declarations before material contracts |
| s.176 | Not accept benefits from third parties | Gifts & hospitality policy (HR) |
| s.177 | Declare interest in proposed transaction | BoardRoom agenda item |

**OBL-063 evidence:** Board minutes template; annual director duties briefing; this programme document.
