**Version:** 1.0.0

## 1. Position

Trancendos is **not** currently an NHS data processor or supplier under an NHS contract. This annex documents **what would be required** if we process NHS patient or staff data, or supply services to NHS trusts under DSPT expectations.

---

## 2. Prerequisites (high level)

| Area | Current state | DSPT gap |
|------|---------------|----------|
| UK GDPR / DPA 2018 | ✅ Operational (Tranc3) + programme | DSPT assertion workbook |
| ICO registration | 🎯 ACT-003 pending | Live registration number on DSPT |
| DPIA / DPIAs | PIA process in place | NHS-specific DPIA templates |
| Access control | IAM, RBAC in Tranc3 | Role matrix for clinical contexts |
| Incident reporting | PROC-IR-001 | NHS 72-hour / Caldicott pathways |
| BCP / DR | PROC-BCP-001; one baseline restore | Evidence for all P0 systems (ACT-012) |
| Supplier assurance | SUP-003–005 DPAs pending | NHS DPA + IG Toolkit clauses |
| Staff training | PROC-TRN-001 | Mandatory IG + Caldicott training |

---

## 3. Trigger checklist

- [ ] NHS trust or ICB contract in pipeline
- [ ] Data categories include patient-identifiable or special-category health data
- [ ] Legal confirms processor/controller roles and DSPT submission party
- [ ] Complete DSPT assertion against National Data Guardian standards
- [ ] Caldicott Guardian or SIRO engagement for customer
