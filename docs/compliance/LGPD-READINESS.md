# LGPD Readiness Annex (Brazil)

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** 2026-06-07  
**Owner:** DPO  
**Legislation:** Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD)  
**Status:** ❌ Not applicable today — ✅ Programme future-readiness

---

## 1. Trigger

Activate this annex when Trancendos:

- Processes personal data of individuals in Brazil, or
- Establishes a Brazilian entity or representative, or
- Targets Brazilian market with localised offering.

---

## 2. LGPD ↔ GDPR control mapping

| LGPD principle | GDPR equivalent | Magna Carta artefact |
|----------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Legal basis (Art. 7) | Art. 6 | ROPA, POL-PRI-001 |
| Data subject rights (Art. 18) | Arts. 15–22 | PROC-DSR-001 |
| DPO (Art. 41) | Art. 37–39 | DPO role in COMPLIANCE-BLUEPRINT |
| Security (Art. 46) | Art. 32 | ISO/SOC2 technical controls |
| International transfer (Art. 33) | Chapter V | SCCs + TIA templates |
| ANPD reporting | ICO/SA notification | PROC-IR-001 adapt for ANPD |

---

## 3. Activation checklist

- [ ] Appoint Brazil representative if required (Art. 41)
- [ ] Register with ANPD if required by guidance
- [ ] Portuguese privacy notice / consent UX
- [ ] ROPA extension for BR processing
- [ ] LGPD clauses in customer and supplier DPAs
- [ ] Impact assessment (RIPD) for high-risk processing
