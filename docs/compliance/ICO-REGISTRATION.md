## 1. Purpose

Guides ICO data protection fee registration for Trancendos as a UK data controller/processor. Registration is a **legal requirement** for organisations processing personal information that are not exempt.

## 2. Applicability assessment

| Question | Trancendos position |
|----------|---------------------|
| Process personal data in UK? | Yes — users, employees, customers |
| Exempt (e.g. only staff admin, courts)? | **No** — platform processes customer/user PII |
| Controller, processor, or both? | **Both** — controller for own operations; processor for customer data on Tranc3 |

**Conclusion:** ICO registration required. Maintain registration details in LEGISLATION-REGISTER regulatory contacts.

---

## 3. Fee tier guidance (2026)

ICO tiers are based on organisation size and turnover. Confirm current fees on [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk).

| Tier | Typical profile | Action |
|------|-----------------|--------|
| Micro | ≤10 staff, ≤£632k turnover | Assess if applicable |
| Small | ≤50 staff, ≤£10.2m turnover | Likely tier for early-stage |
| Medium | ≤250 staff, ≤£36m turnover | Reassess on growth |
| Large | Above medium thresholds | Reassess on growth |

**Owner:** Finance confirms turnover; DPO submits registration.

---

## 4. Registration workflow

1. **Confirm tier** — Finance + DPO (annual reassessment)
2. **Prepare details** — Legal entity name, address, contact, DPO details
3. **Pay fee** — ICO online portal (annual renewal)
4. **Record registration number** — Update LEGISLATION-REGISTER § Regulatory contacts
5. **Display privacy information** — Privacy notice references ICO number when live
6. **Renew** — Calendar reminder 30 days before expiry
