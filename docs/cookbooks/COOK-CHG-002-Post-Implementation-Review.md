# COOK-CHG-002 — Post-Implementation Review Playbook

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** CAB Chair  
**Procedure:** [PROC-CHG-002](../procedures/PROC-CHG-002-Post-Implementation-Review.md)  
**Hymn sheet:** [HYMN-CHG-002](../hymn-sheets/HYMN-CHG-002-PIR-Checklist.md)

---

## When to use

Within 10 business days of any significant or emergency change, or after failed change rollback.

---

## PIR session (30–45 min)

| Step | Question | Outcome |
|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Did change meet objective? | Pass / partial / fail |
| 2 | Incidents during/after? | Link IR tickets |
| 3 | Monitoring gaps? | Update SYSTEMS-REGISTER |
| 4 | Security/compliance impact? | ISMS sign-off |
| 5 | Runbook/cookbook updates needed? | Assign doc owner |

---

## Outputs

1. PIR record attached to change ticket
2. CAPA items if systemic — PROC-CAPA-001
3. Close or extend change record
