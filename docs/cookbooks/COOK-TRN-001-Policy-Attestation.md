# COOK-TRN-001 — Security Awareness & Attestation Playbook

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** HR / ISMS Lead  
**Procedure:** [PROC-TRN-001](../procedures/PROC-TRN-001-Security-Awareness-Attestation.md)  
**Hymn sheet:** [HYMN-TRN-001](../hymn-sheets/HYMN-TRN-001-Attestation-Checklist.md)

---

## When to use

Annual policy attestation cycle, new joiner onboarding (within 14 days), or after major policy revision.

---

## Annual cycle

1. **Publish** policy pack (9 policies) with change summary
2. **Assign** training module + attestation form to all staff/contractors
3. **Track** completion in `compliance/policy_attestation_register.yaml`
4. **Escalate** non-completion to managers at 30 days

---

## New joiner

1. Day 1: Acceptable use + security basics
2. Week 1: Complete attestation before production access (ties to COOK-IAM-001)
3. Log in attestation register

---

## Evidence

Export completion report for PROC-CMP-001 and SOC 2 HR evidence schedule.
