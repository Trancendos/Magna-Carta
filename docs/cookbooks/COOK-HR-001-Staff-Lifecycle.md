# COOK-HR-001 — Staff Lifecycle Playbook

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** HR / ISMS Lead  
**Procedure:** [PROC-HR-001](../procedures/PROC-HR-001-Staff-Lifecycle.md)  
**Hymn sheet:** [HYMN-HR-001](../hymn-sheets/HYMN-HR-001-OnOffboard-Checklist.md)

---

## When to use

Hire, role change, contractor start/end, or privileged role assignment.

---

## Pre-employment

1. Background check per role risk tier (PROC-HR-001 §2)
2. Signed employment/contractor agreement + confidentiality
3. Record screening outcome (no sensitive detail in tickets)

---

## Onboarding

1. HR notifies ISMS with start date and role
2. COOK-IAM-001 access grant after screening clear
3. COOK-TRN-001 attestation within 14 days

---

## Offboarding (same day)

1. **Revoke** all access — auth, VPN, repos, SaaS
2. **Recover** devices and tokens
3. **Confirm** with manager; close ticket
4. Retain HR records per retention policy
