## 4. Privileged role definition

Roles requiring attestation before production access:

- Platform Engineering (deploy)
- Security Ops / ISMS
- DPO and privacy delegates
- AI Lead and model operators
- CAB members with approval authority

Standard staff: POL-SEC-002 (AUP) at onboarding; full pack optional at manager discretion.
