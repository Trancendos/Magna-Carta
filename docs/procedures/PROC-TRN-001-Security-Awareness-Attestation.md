# PROC-TRN-001 — Security Awareness & Policy Attestation

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** 2026-06-07  
**Owner:** HR / ISMS Lead  
**Linked policies:** POL-SEC-001, POL-SEC-002, POL-PRI-001, all operational policies

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensures staff understand security and privacy obligations and formally acknowledge Magna Carta policies. Closes MC-006 staff acknowledgement gap (🎯 operational validation).

---

## 2. Scope

| Audience | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| All employees | AUP (POL-SEC-002) at onboarding |
| Privileged roles | Full policy pack attestation annually |
| Contractors | AUP + relevant policies before system access |

---

## 3. Onboarding

1. HR issues policy links from [policies/INDEX.md](../policies/INDEX.md).
2. Employee completes security awareness module (internal Academy or equivalent).
3. Record attestation in [POLICY-ATTESTATION-REGISTER.md](../evidence/POLICY-ATTESTATION-REGISTER.md).
4. PROC-IAM-001 access provisioning only after AUP attestation for standard roles; full pack for privileged.

---

## 4. Annual cycle

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | ISMS publishes policy version summary if changed | ISMS Lead |
| 2 | HR notifies privileged roles (cycle ATT-YYYY-##) | HR |
| 3 | Attestation via HR system or signed PDF | Employee |
| 4 | Register updated within 5 business days | HR / ISMS |
| 5 | Overdue → manager escalation; access review flag | ISMS Lead |

**2026 cycle:** ATT-2026-01 due 2026-08-31 (ACT-007).

---

## 5. Training topics (minimum)

- Password / MFA hygiene
- Phishing and social engineering
- Data classification and DSR escalation
- Incident reporting (PROC-IR-001)
- AI assistive-use expectations (POL-AI-001)
- Acceptable use and confidential data handling

---

## 6. Records

| Record | Retention |
|--------|-----------|
| Attestation register | 3 years |
| Training completion | 3 years |
